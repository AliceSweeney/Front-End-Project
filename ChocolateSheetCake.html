Instructions

For the cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Grease a 9x13 rectangular pan
  3. In a small dish mix milk an dvinegar together and put aside
  4. In a large saucepan over low heat, melt the butter, cocoa, and water. Don't let it bubble or burn
  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and let cool slightly
  6. To the saucepan add sugar, flour, baking soda, and salt
  7. Mix together with a whisk or wooden spoon
  8. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla and beat quickly to combine
  9. Pour into the pan and bake for 20-25 minutes

For the frosting:

  1. Melt butter over medium heat
  2. Stir in cocoa and milk
  3. Add powdered sugar and vanilla
  4. Beat over low heat until smooth

To assemble:

  1. Pour hot frosting over hot cake
  2. Serve with vanilla ice cream and enjoy!
