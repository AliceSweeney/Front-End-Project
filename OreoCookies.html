  1. Preheat an oven to 350°F (176°C).
  2. Grab a mixing bowl and add the entire cake box mix.
  3. Add the 2 eggs to the mixing bowl.
  4. Add 1/3 cup of oil to mixture.
  5. Grab a silicone spatula or a whisk and mix ingredients together until consistency is...
  6. In a seperate bag or bowl, add 4-6 oreos and crush them but not too crushed, you want some chunks left.
  7. Put in the crushed oreos to the mixture.
  8. Pour in as much white chocolate chips your heart desires.
  9. Combine everything together with a silicone spatula or spoon til everything is mixed evenly.
  10. Grab a cookie pan or anything that's flat and meant for baking and put perchment paper over the entire flat surface.
  11. Grab a spoon and start scooping out the mixture and plot it onto the parchment paper.
  12. Continue doing this throughout the entire flat surface until you think there isn't enough space. You can also add more of the mixture to already placed cookie mixture to make the cookies bigger.
  13. Place the pan into the oven and let it bake for 17 minutes. You can also use a toothpick and poke at the cookies until it comes out clean.
  14. After the cookies are ready take them out the oven and let them cool down for about 5 minutes.
  15. Afterwards, grab a cookie and enjoy!
