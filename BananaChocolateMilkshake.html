Banana Chocolate Milkshake

IMAGE SOURCE: https://irepo.primecp.com/2017/04/325532/Chocolate-Banana-Milkshake_ExtraLarge1000_ID-2174146.jpg?v=2174146

Ingredients Checklist








Instructions List

  1. Grab a blender.
  2. Add 2 cups of milk into the blender.
  3. Split frozen banana in half then add to blender.
  4. Grab a spoon and pour in 2 tablespoons of the Nesquik.
  5. Pour in 2 tablespoons of sugar.
  6. Sprinkle some cinnamon.
  7. Add a couple of ice cubes but not too much. 4-5 ice cubes are a good amount, anything less or more will make the texture not as delicious or will ruin.
  8. Pop on the lid of the blender and mix until everything is thoroughly mixed together.
  9. Pour the milkshake into a cup til almost to the brim.
  10. Grab whipped cream and go crazy with it.
  11. Sprinkle cinnamon all over the whipped cream but not too much otherwise you'll be sneezing.
  12. Pop in a straw and enjoy!
