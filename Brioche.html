Brioche

Ingredients












Instructions

  1. In stand mixer, whisk together milk, yeast, and honey
  2. Wait to see if the yeast is active and becomes foamy
  3. Whisk in eggs and vanilla
  4. Add flour, salt, and sugar and form a shaggy dough
  5. Mix on medium speed for about 4 minutes
  6. Add 1/4 of the butter and mix on medium speed until completely blended
  7. Repeat this step with the rest of the butter, making sure to scrape the bowl every so often
  8. Continue to mix the dough on high speed until it is smooth and pulls away from the sides of the bowl, it should not be sticky
  9. On a floured surface fold the dough into a tight ball
  10. Put back in the mixing bowl and let rise about 1 hour
  11. Take out the dough again and press down to deflate then form into a tight ball
  12. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight
  13. Prep two loaf pans with butter and flour
  14. Remove the dough from the fridge and cut into 2 equal portions
  15. Shape the dough as desired (plain, braided, or shaped into balls in the loaf)
  16. Loosely cover the loaf pans and let rise for two hours or until doubled in size
  17. Optional: brush the tops with egg wash
  18. Preheat oven to 325
  19. Bake for about 40 minutes, the internal temperature should be 190
  20. take bread out and let cool slightly before serving
