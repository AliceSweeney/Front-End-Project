Oreo Mug Cake

IMAGE SOURCE: https://cookingwithkarli.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/oreo-mug-cake-FT.jpg

Ingredients Checklist







Instructions List

  1. Grab any type of mug.
  2. Add 4 oreos into the mug.
  3. Add the milk.
  4. Grab a fork or spoon and fully smash the oreos. DO NOT LEAVE ANY CHUNKS!
  5. Add the sugar.
  6. Add the flour.
  7. Add the baking powder.
  8. Add the cacao powder. -- IF YOU DON'T HAVE CACAO POWDER, USE NESQUIK POWDER AS A SUBSTITUTE --
  9. Mix everything together.
  10. Add the final oreo at the bottom of the mug. -- THE OREO MUST BE COVERED, NOT CAPABLE OF BEING SEEN --
  11. Microwave for 1 minute.
  12. Take it out of microwave and enjoy. -- WARNING: REALLY HOT SO LET COOL DOWN FOR ABOUT 2 MINUTES OR SO --
