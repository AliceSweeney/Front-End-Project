Instructions List

  1. In a large skillet, saute the onions in olive oil until softened. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook. Stir occasionally for 30 minutes for until deep goldren brown.
  2. Let onions cool entirely and add balsamic vinegar or honey.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the sour cream, yogurt, onion powder, and salt. Stir in two-thirds of the coooked onions.
  4. Garnish the mixture.
  5. Transfer dip into a serving bowl, top it with remaining inions.
  6. Serve with chips and/or vegatables and enjoy!
