Taquitos Dorados

IMAGE SOURCE: https://i.ytimg.com/vi/rSLhwWRyPUk/maxresdefault.jpg

Ingredients Checklist








Instructions List

  1. Mix the shredded meat with the salsa or hot sauce, breaking up any large pieces. -- You need to roll a delicate tortilla around this and you don't want no big lumpy bits --
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat for about 4 minutes. Using your hands(or tongs) dip the tortillas in the hot oil for a few seconds to soften.
  3. Set them in a tray or plate.
  4. Put a tablespoon or 2 of the shredded meat in a thin rectangle on the first third of a tortilla.
  5. Wrap it up tightly but gently, as the tortillas are tender.
  6. Slide the taquito, seam side down, aside. Repeat with the remaining taquitos.
  7. Heat the oil to at least 325F/ideally 350F. Using a thin spatula, slip the end o the spatula under the seam of a taquito and carefully place it into the hot oil, seam side down.
  8. Press on the top of the taquito for 10 seconds to hold it. Repeat with a few more taquitos. -- You'll need to do this in batches so you can flip them --
  9. Set a cooling rack on a baking sheet and put that in the oven, setting the oven to "keep warm". -- You can also just place them on a huge place with napkins under --
  10. After 1-2 minutes, turn your taquitos over and fry them on the other side for another 1-2mintues.
  11. Move them to the rack in the oven and repeat with the remaining taquitos.
  12. Serve with the salsa. cheese, sour cream, or whaever you prefer.
