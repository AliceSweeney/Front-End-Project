Instructions List

Mozzarella Sticks

  1. In one dish, combine flour, salt, and pepper and whisk to combine.
  2. In another dish, combine eggs and milk and whisk to beat and combine.
  3. In a third dish, combine all seasonings and bread crumbs. Whisk to comine.
  4. Working with one piece of cheese at a time, dredge each piece of string cheese in flour mixture, shake off excess.
  5. Dip in the egg mixture.
  6. Coat in the bread crumb mixture, pressing to ensure it sticks.
  7. Repeat this process again for each stick, then transfer to a prepared baking sheet. Freeze for at least 1 hour.
  8. Fill a large pot with 2" oil over medium heat until a thermometer inserted in the oil temperature reaches 350Â°F. Working in batches no more than 4.
  9. Carefully drop sticks into hot oil and turn occasionally until golden brown and crisp (roughly 2 minutes).
  10. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate, and sprinkle with a little salt if desired.

Mariana Sauce

  1. Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Then add garlic and saute until fragrant (1-2 minutes).
  2. Add tomatoes, basil, oregano, and salt. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally for 10 minutes.
  3. Add red pepper then stir and simmer another minute.
  4. Transfer to serving dish.
