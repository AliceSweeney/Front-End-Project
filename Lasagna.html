Lasagna

Image Source:https://thehungrybluebird.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/IMG_3213.jpg.webp

Ingredients

For the Ragu:







For the pasta:

1 package of lasagna

For the Bechamel:


1/2 cup of flour

Salt and pepper to taste
Nutmeg to taste

Instructions

For the Ragu:

  1. In a large pot, break up the turkey and cook until done
  2. While the turkey is cooking, alternate between mixing it and chopping your other ingredients
  3. Remove the turkey and set aside
  4. Put onions, carrots, and celery in the pot and stir until they are somewhat softer
  5. Add in the garlic, tomato sauce, and turkey
  6. Let the pot simmer on low heat for at least an hour or until the flavor is good

For the pasta

  1. Follow package instructions to cook the pasta
  2. Lay pasta sheets out on baking pans and lightly cover in olive oil to prevent sticking

For the Bechamel:

  1. In a medium saucepan melt the butter
  2. Add in flour an stir until well combined with few clumps
  3. Heat up milk in the microwave until it is hard to put your finger in
  4. Slowly add in milk while stirring
  5. Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste

Assembly:

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Get a large baking dish
  3. Spread a layer of ragu
  4. Spread a layer of pasta
  5. Spread a layer of bechamel
  6. Continue these steps until you run out of space or materials
  7. Add a final layer of mozzarella on top
  8. Bake until the cheese on top is browned and melty
